## Overview

NodePress is dual-licensed:

- **Community Edition:** GPL-3.0-or-later (see [LICENSE](LICENSE))
- **Commercial Edition:** This license — for proprietary, closed-source, or enterprise use

## When do you need a Commercial License?

You need a commercial license if:

- You are building a proprietary product on top of NodePress and cannot open-source your modifications
- You are building SaaS infrastructure where GPL obligations conflict with your business model
- You are distributing NodePress as part of a commercial product to end users

## What does the Community (GPL) license allow?

Under GPL-3.0:

- Use NodePress freely for any purpose, including commercial services
- Modify and distribute the source code
- Build plugins and themes — **they inherit GPL** and must also be open source

## Contributor License Agreement

All contributors to NodePress must sign the Contributor License Agreement (CLA) before their contributions can be accepted. The CLA grants the NodePress project the right to distribute contributions under both the GPL community license and the commercial license.

CLA Assistant is configured on this repository. You will be prompted to sign automatically when you open a pull request.
